# 5-minute pitch script skeleton (~4:30–5:00 with buffer)

**Markings:** *(pause)* **bold** = emphasis. Time each block out loud.

---

## 1. Hook (~15 sec)

*(Personal story or sharp stat that ties YOU to the problem.)*  
[ ]

---

## 2. Problem (~45 sec)

Who hurts, how badly, how often, what they do today:  
[ ]

**Transition:** “That’s the gap we’re solving.”

---

## 3. Solution (~45 sec)

What you built, how it works in one clear arc (demo line if product in hand):  
[ ]

**Transition:** “Here’s why this can be a real business.”

---

## 4. Market (~30 sec)

TAM → SAM → SOM; **beachhead** in plain English:  
[ ]

**Transition:** “We’re not the only option — here’s why we still win.”

---

## 5. Competition (~30 sec)

Landscape + wedge **beyond price** (insight, workflow, trust, speed):  
[ ]

**Transition:** “We’re not guessing — here’s what we’ve learned from real people.”

---

## 6. Traction / Validation (~30 sec)

Evidence stack (method + numbers):  
[ ]

**Transition:** “Here’s how we make money.”

---

## 7. Business Model (~30 sec)

Pricing, gross margin story, unit economics hint:  
[ ]

**Transition:** “And here’s how we get it to customers.”

---

## 8. Go-to-Market (~20 sec)

Phase 1 → 2 → 3 (beachhead → expand → scale):  
[ ]

**Transition:** “If you back us, here’s exactly where the money goes.”

---

## 9. Use of Funds (~20 sec)

Categories with **$ and %**, tied to milestones:  
[ ]

**Transition:** “Why us — briefly.”

---

## 10. Team (~15 sec)

Founder–problem fit + missing skill + how you fill it:  
[ ]

**Transition:** “Close.”

---

## 11. Ask + Close (~10 sec)

**The ask:** [funding amount / intros / pilot partners — be specific]  
**One-liner they’ll remember:**  
[ ]

---

### Q&A reminders (practice longer than the pitch)

- For every number: how did you validate it?  
- For every claim: what would a judge poke at first?  
See `references/judge-question-bank.md`.
