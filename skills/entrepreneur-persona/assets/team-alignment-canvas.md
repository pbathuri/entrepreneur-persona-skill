# Team alignment canvas

## Instructions

For co-founder teams (2+ people). Each founder completes independently first, then compare and reconcile.
Disagreements are data — surface them early, not during a crisis.

---

## Section E: Conflict protocol

- **How will you handle disagreements?** (Mediator, advisor tie-break, structured discussion, majority vote)
- **Check-in cadence:** [ weekly / biweekly ] co-founder meeting with explicit agenda
- **Exit terms:** What happens to equity, IP, responsibilities, and customer relationships if a founder leaves?
- **Buyout mechanism:** Is there a pre-agreed valuation method or right of first refusal?

---

**Done when:** Every cell is filled, every disagreement is discussed, and the founding team can articulate the same vision in their own words.
